History of Sylhet Nagri

Sylhet Nagri, also known as Sylheti Nagri, is an ancient script integral to the culture of the Sylhet region in Bangladesh. Through Sylhet Nagri, generations have conveyed stories, emotions, and history, connecting communities with a sense of shared identity.

Learn About Nagri

The Nagri script is a phonetic alphabet unique to the greater Sylhet, holding historical significance and cultural identity.
