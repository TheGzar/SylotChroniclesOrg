History of Sylhet Nagri

Sylhet Nagri, also known as Sylheti Nagri, is an ancient script that has been integral to the culture and heritage of the Sylhet region in Bangladesh. Dating back centuries, it was used primarily for writing poetic and literary works, preserving folk stories and spiritual texts. The script's simplicity and phonetic structure made it accessible to the local population, and it holds significant cultural and historical value. Through Sylhet Nagri, generations have conveyed stories, emotions, and history, connecting communities with a sense of shared identity and heritage.
